# Sharing and Permissions

## Share link expiration
Share links default to a 30-day expiration on Free and Pro plans. Team plan admins can set links to never expire via Settings > Sharing Defaults.

## Recipient can't open a shared file
The two most common causes: (1) the link expired, (2) the recipient is trying to open it in a country where our CDN is geo-restricted (currently affects a small set of embargoed regions). Ask the recipient to confirm the exact error shown.

## Transferring folder ownership
Folder ownership transfer is only available on the Team plan, via Settings > Team > Transfer Ownership. On Free/Pro, the workaround is to share the folder with full edit access and have the new owner make a copy.

## Permission changes not taking effect
Permission changes can take up to 5 minutes to propagate to all collaborators' clients due to caching. If it's been longer than 15 minutes, a manual cache flush is needed on our end.
