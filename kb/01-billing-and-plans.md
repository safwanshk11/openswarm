# Billing and Plans

Loomly Cloud has three plans: Free (5GB), Pro ($9/mo, 500GB), and Team ($29/mo per seat, 2TB shared).

## Refunds
Refunds are issued automatically for cancellations within 14 days of a charge. After 14 days, refunds require manual approval from billing support and are only granted for double-charges or failed service.

## Downgrades
Downgrading from Pro or Team takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Data over the new plan's storage limit is not deleted, but uploads are blocked until usage is brought under the limit.

## Failed payments
If a card fails, we retry 3 times over 5 days. After the third failure the account is moved to read-only mode (no new uploads or shares) but existing data remains accessible.

## Invoices
Invoices are available under Settings > Billing > Invoice History. We cannot email invoices to addresses other than the account owner's for security reasons.
